Dear Bash,

I get so lost in my love for you

I often type pwd to discover where I am again.

Or, perhaps I choose to cd ~ to get back to my home directory,

instead of typing cd .. many times.


And when I want to make a new file,

all it takes is a simple touch.

Or, I can type mkdir, and get a brand new folder.


Nothing lasts forever,

mostly because nano let's me edit.

Just know I'll never rm you,

even though you can sometimes cd me into a bad place.


You're always up to date on the latest trends,

especially when I -v to make sure.

I also love how you're open and share everything with me,

because I can read you with just a simple less,

and freely move windows with control <- ->.


The only way to grow my love for you

would be to duplicate it, and then typing ssh, followed by my identikey@creative.colorado.edu.

And not to forget, I would enter my password like the key you have to my heart.

Then, in my other terminal,

I'd type scp, followed by my file name, where I want the file to go,

like (tysc0175@creative.colorado.edu):public_html/dev.


Even though our love is through a terminal,

I know it will never terminate.


Tyler :)
